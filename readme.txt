📌 Project Overview

MedTrackFit is a community-driven health recovery platform designed to make healing affordable and guided.
 
It connects:

🤕 Sufferer Patients

💚 Recovered Patients

🧭 Health Mentors

🧑‍⚕️ Doctors

The platform ensures that patients receive structured recovery support, real-life experience guidance, and professional supervision when required.

🎯 Problem Statement

Many middle-class and lower-income patients cannot afford continuous doctor consultations.

Patients often feel alone during long recovery journeys.

Recovered patients have valuable healing experience but lack a structured platform to guide others.

There is no affordable system for continuous recovery monitoring.

💡 Solution

MedTrackFit creates a 4-role recovery ecosystem:

1️⃣ Sufferer Patient

Searches for help

Connects with recovered patients or mentors

Tracks health metrics

Books doctor consultation if needed

2️⃣ Recovered Patient

Shares real-life healing journey

Guides sufferers through structured recovery steps

Earns by helping others

3️⃣ Health Mentor

Connects sufferers with the right recovered patient

Monitors progress

Ensures accountability

4️⃣ Doctor

Intervenes if recovery is not progressing

Verifies treatment plans

Provides professional medical advice

🚀 Key Features

🔐 Role-based authentication system

👤 Profile management

🔗 Connect / Connected status system

📊 Health dashboard with:

Health Score

Goal Progress

Active Connections

Upcoming Appointments

🧘 Meditation timer

💧 Water intake tracker

🥗 Nutrition score tracking

🔍 Search & filter system

🌙 Dark/Light mode support
